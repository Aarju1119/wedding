Welcome to SnapForever Studios! We are a boutique Candid Wedding Photography Studio based in Agra, India and are known for a fun, off-beat and modern approach to creating stories from the best day in your life. Led by Aarju & Ayush Shrivastava, Our Parent started photographing Indian weddings in 1990, and since then have created hundreds of unique love stories across the world. We are passionate, hard-working and an incredibly motivated team; we love Indian weddings, and can’t wait to be a part of yours!
